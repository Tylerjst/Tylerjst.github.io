Coming to college is exciting.

Figuring out what to bring can be stressful though. As a communications intern in the Office of Housing Operations, I created a move in list for incoming first year students so they could get an idea of some sustainable options they could choose from. The list ended up being posted in the campus Barnes and Noble with sustainable items in the bookstore being designated with OSI's oak leaf logo. The move in list was then followed up with a series of infographics that explained what made some of the items sustainable.
