Sustainable Promotions Public and Educational Outreach

Teaching about sustainability

isn't easy. As an intern for the Office of Residence Life and for the Office of Sustainability Initiatives, I saw an opportunity to merge roles and address misconceptions and misinformation by creating a Sustainable Move-In List for incoming first-years. This list suggests items to buy when moving into college, but designates sustainable options with the OSI Oak leaf. By partnering with the campus Barnes and Noble, the list was also published on the BnN website and sustainable options in the store were again marked with the OSI Oak Leaf. The intent was to link the well-designed Oak Leaf with some sort of sustainable option, so that if someone were to see the logo anywhere, they would automatically think about sustainability. The list was also paired with a series of infographics to further explain some sustainable topics.

TL;DR:

  • Created a Sustainable Move-In List
  • Partnered with On-Campus Barnes & Noble
  • Created a series of Sustainability Infographics
  • Help promote a recognizable 'brand' to identify sustainable choices
