10-Year Sustainability Report 10 years of OSI at Emory

Sustainability at Emory

wasn't really a thing until a short while ago; 2005 to be exact. That's when The Office of Sustainability Iniatives, or OSI, was founded. Since then, they've been hard at work setting sustainability goals for the university and promoting student engagment in sustainability efforts. As an intern for their office, I created an html report highlighting some of their accomplishments and work since their inception. This project continued my goal to help redesign sustainability at Emory so that people could connect it with trendy, minimalist design that could be something cool to support.
