Video Production
Student Parking & Last Minute Casting

I've been making movies since I was in middle school.

Filmmaking combines everything that I love: writing, comedy, and having fun with a team of partners. I continued making and studying films as an undergrad, working with some friends to create films such as If Else. This was our submission for Campus Movie Fest, dubbing ourselves Last Minute Casting. After I graduated, I decided to take some of my class projects to start my own channel: Student Parking. I'm now using this channel as a hub for the video projects that pop up, including Emory's Creating Emory orientation video and ECO's highlights of COP22 in Marrakech, Morocco.

There's nothing I've found that matches the joy of collaborative filmmaking.
