Video Production

Filmmaking combines everything that I love: writing, comedy, and having fun with a team. I made and studied films in undergrad, working with friends to create movies like If Else. After I graduated, I decided to take some of my class projects to start my own channel: Student Parking. I'm now using this channel as a hub for the video projects that pop up, including Emory's Creating Emory orientation video and ECO's highlights of COP22 in Marrakech, Morocco.
