Visual Design

I started using Photoshop as a toy, faceswapping my friends and making absurd dioramas.

Soon after, I learned my crazy ideas could make for some engaging advertisements and flyers. After about four years of trial and error, my Photoshop skills expanded into Illustrator and InDesign and my work began incorporating more serious work: Facebook banners, flyers, and logos for organizations.

I still like to find ways to add my charm.
