Communication & Comedy

Ever since I realized that comedy involved deliberate writing, I became interested in the power of language.

I started with Storieshouse.com, a blog that my friends and I made in 2012. It was at first a way to share stories with each other as we all went off to college, but soon we began posting our attempts at satire, analysis, and fiction. I then moved onto writing satire more seriously [irony] for The Emory Spoke, Emory's satirical magazine. As I refined my poop jokes, my understanding and interest in constructing language increased. I enjoy finding ways to communicate information effectively, normally including humor. I find that using comedy is the best way to engage people, whether it be to learn something or think a different way. Evoking that emotional response is the surest way to encourage others to continue seeking it out.

Plus it's way more fun.
