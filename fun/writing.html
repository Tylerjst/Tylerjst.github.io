Professional Writing

Ever since I realized that comedy involved deliberate writing, I became interested in the power of language.

I started writing fiction and satire for Storieshouse.com, a blog that my friends and I made in 2012. Soon, I moved onto writing satire more seriously [irony] for The Emory Spoke, Emory's satirical magazine.

I enjoy finding ways to communicate information effectively while including humor. I find that comedy is the best way to engage people, especially when teaching them something new. By making learning fun, it is more likely that others will want to continue to learn.
