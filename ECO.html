Emory Climate Organization Climate Activism at Emory

Emory Climate Organization,

or ECO for short, is a charted organization at Emory University that seeks to augment climate literacy and activism on campus. The organization initially started as a project in my Paris is an Explanation class. This Interdisclinary Studies (IDS) class was a joint collaboration with the Goizueta Business School and Emory College's Environmental Science and Journalism departments to study, and attend, the climate change negotiations occuring at COP21 in Paris, France. Once our delegation returned, we decided to mobilize on our campus with the intention of creating a construct that would continue to fight for climate action beyond our undergraduate experience.
