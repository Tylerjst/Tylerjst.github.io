II. Gaining Momentum

In Spring 2017, we created a fundraising campaign to raise money for future delegations to COP. This was important because funding for this program was beginning to dwindle, and many students in the past relied on that financial aid to attend the conference.

Momentum Sticker

A flyer for Climate Week 2016.

In order to give future students the opportunity that I was fortunate enough to receive, I helped to develop marketing and communication strategies in our fundraising efforts.

More specifically, I used my footage I shot from COP22 video to create a video series of 6, 30 second shorts to be used in email blasts. These showcased highlights from the conference to portray the experience to potential donors.

Furthermore, I came up with the idea of creating a limited edition sticker to be sent to donors at the conclusion of the fundraiser. This gift, borrowing some structure from kickstarters campaigns, would hopefuly entice some people into donating even just a little bit.

At the end of our campaign, we exceeded our goal, raising over $3,000.

III. Giving thanks

At the conclusion of our fundraising campaign, we wanted to thank everyone who had donated. We prepared thank you notes on recycled paper and mailed them, along with the stickers, to each of the 30+ donors. The language of the thank you note was crafted together while I designed the physical letters, including the new UN Climate Delegation featured on the back.
