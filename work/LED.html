LEDs around Emory Implementing Sustainable Lighting (and the future)

I like LEDs

maybe a little bit too much; My roommate and I bought a pair of multicolored LED string lights a couple of years ago because we wanted to make our dorm room look like it was straight out of Tron. I find the tiny semi-conductors so cool because they're an elegant solution for sustainability challenges. They embody the fusion of technology with a futuristic, minimalist design, which is the core of who I am. When I noticed that my university was continuing to use T8 CFLs in the vast majority of their buildings, I applied for a grant to replace them with more efficient LEDs to contribute to their goal of reducing energy consumption while spreading the aesthetic that I've come to cherish.

Check out my grant application.
