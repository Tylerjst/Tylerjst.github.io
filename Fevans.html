In the fall of 2014,

I served as a resident adivsor in Few and Evans hall. This residence hall was home to the Living Green Living-Learning Community, where first year residents learned how to lead sustainable lifestyles in the 21st century. I was tasked with creating a flyer that depicted the residence hall's mission statement. Feeling inspired, I went ahead and created a logo for the hall while I was at it.
