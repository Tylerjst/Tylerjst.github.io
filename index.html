About

I'm a recent graduate from Emory University with a B.S. in Applied Physics. I have a wide set of interests that span astronomy and physics, sustainability, philosophy, design, film, writing, and music. The dream is to develop well-designed, sustainable infrastructure and rethink educational systems to encourage more inquisitiveness and critical inquiry. I'm the kind of person who likes to think about big ideas. I'm also the kind of person who likes to watch an ungodly amount of YouTube videos and make silly noises with my mouth.

People often call this "beatboxing," and are now mistaking it for talent.
