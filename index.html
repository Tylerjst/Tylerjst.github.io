About

I'm a recent graduate from Emory University with a B.S. in Applied Physics. I have a wide set of interests that span astronomy and physics, sustainability, philosophy, design, film, writing, and music. The dream is to develop well-designed, sustainable infrastructure and rethink educational systems to encourage more inquisitiveness and critical inquiry. I'm the kind of person who likes to think about big ideas.

I'm the kind of person who watches ungodly amount of YouTube videos and makes silly noises with my mouth. I have a wide set of interests that span satire, lists, non-sequiter, and my grandma's family recipe for meatballs provided they don't interfere with my love of run-on sentences. My sense of humour is strange, but I tend to think that humor is (most often) the best way to communicate ideas to people. I've come to trust my bizzare intuiton; when equipped with the analytical part of my brain, I've found that I can harness the absurdity into something productive and slightly less weird (maybe).

E.g. SNL Digital Shorts, which define my middle and high school experience, and their creative (read: bizzare) blend of filmmaking, comedy, and music production embody the artistic fields that evoke sheer joy. In addition, People frequently call my silly mouth noises "beatboxing," and are now mistaking it for talent.
